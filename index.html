About Us

Cunningham Lawn & Landscaping has been a established company since 2013 in the greater Volusia County area, covering Ormond Beach, Daytona Beach, Port Orange and New Smyrna Beach and the entire West Volusia area. Our traditional business model is to provide landscape maintenance services to commercial and residential properties in our area. We also provide full landscape design and installation services, horticulture care, along with landscape lighting and a full-service irrigation department. We aim to be the one company that can do it all. Add to it, that we have a on-staff horticulturist as well as landscape designers, we truly have the capability to perform concept to completion and beyond for all your landscape needs.

Our Services

What we are best at

Grounds Maintenance

A big part of what we do here at Cunningham Lawn & Landscape is lawn maintenance. Our dedicated teams ensure that we are there at your property each in every week in growing season to keep the fresh crisp look your lawn deserves. Our teams are cross trained to also do the pruning of your shrubs while we are onsite as well. Proper pruning and mowing techniques help ensure your property has long term health and success with all your living material.

Horticulture Care

With our on-staff horticulturist, we make sure your lawn and shrubs stay healthy and vibrant in tough Florida weather conditions. When building your customer horticulture care program, we’ll be working with our irrigation staff in unison to insure the property gets the proper water as well as the correct nutritional needs for the plants and turfgrass.

Irrigation

The most important part of plant health is the correct amount of irrigation. Without the right amount of irrigation, plants and turf will struggle to be healthy for the long term. We also make sure that in the rainy season, that the irrigation systems are only working on a “as-needed” basis, as mother nature takes over. From timers to valves and the irrigation heads, we have the entire system covered.

Landscape Design & Installation

Our team of designers will design with the proper “right plant in the right place, does the right thing” focus. With input from our homeowners and commercial property owners, we like to deliver the new long-term solution for the newest of landscapes. Our designers take input from the customer, as well as the staff horticulturist and director of irrigation. So, it’s a team effort in putting a new landscape installation package together.

Hardscapes

From pavers to retaining walls to a lush backyard water feature, our hardscape teams here at Cunningham Lawn & Landscape are trained professionals that love their work. Whether you need a small patio or a entire driveway, we can install it for you. Our sales team encourages our customers to be engaged in selecting style and color as well as patterns. We also do outdoor fire pits, kitchens and seat walls for more entertainment space. Let us know how we can help you!

Sod

As one of the largest sod installers in the area, we specialize in installation of various types of sod. From the St. Augustine that we see on most properties, to Zoysia or Bermuda. We also perform removal, grading and installation in one bulk pricing. Let us know what kind of sod you’d like installed.
